Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use Gigtick?

No. Gigtick works fully offline with no account or sign-up. You can start adding concerts right away.

Where is my data stored?

Locally on your device. Your concerts, achievements, notes, and the references to your photos and videos are kept in the app on your iPhone — nothing is uploaded to our servers.

Why does the app ask for access to my Photos?

For two things: Smart Import, which finds likely past-concert photo clusters on your device, and adding media to a concert. When you attach a photo or video, Gigtick stores a reference to the original in your Photos library rather than duplicating it, so the app stays small.

If I delete a photo or video inside Gigtick, is it removed from my Photos library?

No. Deleting media in the app only removes Gigtick's reference (and any offline copy you downloaded). The original in your Photos library is never touched.

How do I back up my concerts?

Your data is included in your iCloud device backup (Settings → your name → iCloud → iCloud Backup). Restoring a new iPhone from that backup brings your concerts back. Live sync across devices is planned for a future update.

I downloaded videos for offline — where do they live?

Downloaded videos are stored on your device so they play instantly without internet, and they stay until you remove them. You can see the size and clear them anytime in Settings → Downloaded.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual concerts in the app, or remove everything at once with Settings → Delete all data. Deleting the app from your iPhone also removes all of its data.

Why don't my photos show on another device?

Photos and videos added as Photos-library references travel between your devices through iCloud Photos. If iCloud Photos is turned off, those items may not appear on a second device. Videos you explicitly downloaded for offline are kept by the app itself and always play.

Privacy

Gigtick does not track you and includes no ads or analytics SDKs. Read the full Privacy Policy for details on what the app does and doesn't do with your data.
